The Gapminder Bubble Chart

GDP per capita vs. technology adoption. Each bubble is a country — sized by population, colored by region. Animated over 64 years.

▶ Press Play to animate ↔ Drag slider to scrub 🖱 Hover for details 🔍 Click legend to filter

How to Use This Chart

Y-axis: Toggle between Internet Users (%), Mobile Subscriptions (per 100), and Fixed Broadband (per 100). X-axis: GDP per capita (PPP) with a logarithmic scale — toggle to linear if preferred. Animation: Press Play to watch countries move up and right over time, tracing the path of development and technology adoption. Trails: Enable to see each country's 15-year trajectory. Region Filter: Click any region in the legend to isolate it, or cycle through all regions with the highlight button.
