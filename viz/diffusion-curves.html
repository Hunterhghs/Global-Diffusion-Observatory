Understanding S-Curves

The S-curve (logistic function) is the signature pattern of technology diffusion. Adoption starts slowly among innovators (2.5%), accelerates through early adopters (13.5%) and early majority (34%), then slows as the late majority (34%) and laggards (16%) adopt. The steepness and timing of the curve reveals a country's position in the global diffusion cascade.
